## What This Project Is

Example Showcase Company is a placeholder identity used to demonstrate a complete showcase website.

The starter keeps real website structure: homepage sections, product or service examples, an about page, contact flow, legal pages, Storybook, design tokens, rules, tests, and deployment scripts.

The content is intentionally replaceable. It should help you judge layout and quality, not act as a real business claim.

## What You Replace

### Brand Facts

Replace the company name, legal name, tagline, domain, email, phone, address, and social links.

### Offerings

Replace the example product and service categories with the real offer your project needs to present.

### Proof

Replace placeholder certifications, metrics, customer references, process claims, images, and documents with verified evidence.

### Deployment

Replace Cloudflare account settings, domain routes, form receivers, email service credentials, Turnstile keys, and other secrets.

## Why This Starter Exists

AI can create pages quickly, but without governance it may duplicate components, hard-code styles, scatter copy, or forget verification.

This starter keeps the reusable pieces in place:

- component governance;
- Storybook preview;
- design token rules;
- content ownership rules;
- validation commands;
- Cloudflare/OpenNext deployment path;
- Codex and Claude workflow entrypoints.

## How to Use It

1. Read `docs/website/README.md`.
2. Follow `docs/website/新项目替换清单.md`.
3. Replace brand and content surfaces.
4. Preview components and pages.
5. Run validation before launch.
